How to Reply to “Will Review” Professionally

“Will review” usually confirms receipt, not completion. In most cases, no response is required unless timing, expectations, or missing context need clarification.

The replies below help you acknowledge the update without sounding pushy or redundant.

Copy & Paste Replies

Neutral

Thank you. I look forward to your feedback.

Deadline

Thanks. Please let me know if you need anything further before the deadline.

Client-Safe

Thank you for the update. I’ll wait for your thoughts.

Internal

Sounds good. Thanks.

Clarifying

Understood. Just to confirm, do you also need the supporting files?

Closing

Great, thank you.
