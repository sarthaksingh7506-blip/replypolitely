How to Reply to “Seen” Professionally

In workplace chat tools like Slack, Teams, or WhatsApp, a message being marked as “Seen” without a reply can be unclear. If you need a response, the goal is to follow up calmly—without pressure or assumptions.

Copy & Paste Replies

Neutral

Just checking in to see if you had a chance to review this.

Professional

I noticed you saw my message. Please let me know if you need any additional information from me.

Follow-Up

Gentle follow-up on this. Happy to clarify if needed.

Internal Team

Just making sure this didn’t get buried. Do we have a decision?

Client-Safe

I wanted to ensure you received this. Please let me know if there’s anything else you need.

Final Nudge

I’ll assume no further action is needed unless I hear otherwise. Thank you.
