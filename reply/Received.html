How to Reply to “Received” Professionally

A one-word email saying “Received” is efficient, but it can feel abrupt or like a dead end. In similar situations—such as replying to “Noted”— you may want to confirm next steps, add clarity, or close the conversation more professionally.

Copy & Paste Replies

Neutral

Thanks for confirming.

Confirming Action

Great. I’ll proceed with the next phase and update you by Friday.

Professional Close

Glad it reached you safely. Have a great week.

Client-Safe

Thank you for confirming. Please let me know if you have any questions.

Internal

Thanks. I’ll archive this on my end.

Formal

Acknowledged. Thank you for the update.
