How to Follow Up When Someone Doesn’t Respond to an Email

No response does not always mean rejection. Messages get missed, buried, or deprioritized. A polite follow-up keeps things moving without sounding impatient or desperate.

Copy & Paste Replies

Gentle Nudge

Just following up in case this got buried. No rush — I’d appreciate your thoughts when you have a moment.

Second Follow-Up

Circling back on this. Please let me know if you have any questions or need additional information.

Deadline Driven

Following up to ensure we stay on track. Please let me know if this can be reviewed by tomorrow.

Client-Safe

I wanted to confirm you received my message. Please let me know if there’s anything I can clarify.

Final Nudge

I haven’t heard back, so I’ll assume this isn’t a priority right now. I’ll close this on my end, but feel free to reconnect anytime.
